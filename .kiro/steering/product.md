# Product Overview

## Purpose

Influencer-Company Intelligent Matching Platform - a professional B2B collaboration SaaS that connects influencers with companies through data-driven compatibility scoring.

**This is NOT a dating app.** This is a collaboration intelligence platform for professional partnerships.

## Target Users

1. **Influencers**: Content creators seeking brand partnerships aligned with their niche, audience, and engagement metrics
2. **Companies**: Brands looking for influencers that match campaign goals, budget, and target audience
3. **Admins**: Platform administrators managing users and system operations

## Key Features

- **Intelligent Matching Engine**: Weighted scoring algorithm based on niche, location, budget, platform, audience size, and engagement
- **Role-Based Dashboards**: Unique experiences for Influencers, Companies, and Admins
- **Advanced Filtering**: Fast, query-optimized filtering by multiple criteria
- **Real-Time Messaging**: WebSocket-based communication for collaboration discussions
- **Profile Management**: Rich profiles with portfolio media, metrics, and preferences
- **Collaboration Tracking**: Campaign invitations and partnership management

## Product Principles

- Data-driven decision making
- Professional SaaS-grade experience
- Performance and scalability first
- Enterprise trust and security
- Modular and maintainable architecture
