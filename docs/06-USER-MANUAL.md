## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit the Platform**
   - Go to the homepage
   - Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"

2. **Choose Your Role**
   - Select "Influencer" if you create content
   - Select "Company" if you're looking for influencers

3. **Enter Your Information**
   - Email address
   - Password (must be at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
   - Full name
   - Accept terms and conditions

4. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click the verification link
   - You'll be redirected to complete your profile

### Completing Your Profile

A complete profile increases your chances of finding great matches!

**For Influencers:**
- Upload a profile photo
- Write a compelling bio
- Add your location
- Select your niches/industries
- Connect your social media accounts
- Add follower counts and engagement rates
- Upload portfolio items (photos, videos, case studies)

**For Companies:**
- Upload company logo
- Write company description
- Add your industry
- Specify company size
- Set your budget range
- Define target audience
- Describe collaboration goals

#### Understanding Compatibility Scores

Your compatibility score (0-100%) is calculated based on:
- **Niche Alignment (30%):** How well your content matches their industry
- **Budget Match (20%):** If their budget fits your rates
- **Location (15%):** Geographic proximity
- **Platform Overlap (15%):** Shared social media platforms
- **Audience Size (10%):** Follower count alignment
- **Engagement (10%):** Your engagement rate

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Can't log in?**
1. Check email and password
2. Ensure email is verified
3. Try "Forgot Password"
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try different browser

**Forgot password?**
1. Click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Log in with new password

### Profile Issues

**Profile not showing in matches?**
1. Complete your profile (100%)
2. Verify email address
3. Ensure profile is active
4. Check privacy settings
5. Wait 24 hours for indexing

**Can't upload images?**
1. Check file size (max 10MB)
2. Use supported formats (JPG, PNG, WebP)
3. Check internet connection
4. Try different browser
5. Contact support

### Messaging Issues

**Messages not sending?**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
5. Check if user blocked you

**Not receiving notifications?**
1. Check notification settings
2. Check email spam folder
3. Enable browser notifications
4. Check email preferences
5. Verify email address

### Payment Issues

**Payment failed?**
1. Check payment method
2. Ensure sufficient funds
3. Verify billing address
4. Try different card
5. Contact your bank

**Haven't received payout?**
1. Check Stripe Connect setup
2. Verify bank account
3. Check payout schedule
4. Review payment status
5. Contact support

### General Issues

**Page not loading?**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try incognito mode
5. Try different browser

**Feature not working?**
1. Check if feature is enabled
2. Refresh page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Check browser console for errors
5. Contact support

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the platform free to use?**
A: Yes, creating an account and browsing matches is free. Payment processing fees apply to transactions.

**Q: How is my compatibility score calculated?**
A: Based on niche alignment, budget match, location, platform overlap, audience size, and engagement rate.

**Q: How long does it take to find matches?**
A: Matches are generated within 24 hours of completing your profile.

**Q: Can I work with multiple brands/influencers?**
A: Yes, there's no limit to the number of collaborations you can have.

**Q: How do payments work?**
A: Payments are processed securely through Stripe. Companies pay via credit card, influencers receive payouts via Stripe Connect.

**Q: What if a collaboration doesn't work out?**
A: Communicate with your partner first. If unresolved, contact support for mediation.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent.

**Q: How do I verify my social media accounts?**
A: Connect your accounts in Profile Edit. We'll verify follower counts automatically.

**Q: What happens to my data?**
A: We protect your data according to our Privacy Policy. You can export or delete your data anytime.

**Q: Can I use the platform on mobile?**
A: Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works on all devices. A mobile app is planned.

## Glossary

**Compatibility Score:** AI-calculated match percentage between influencer and company

**Collaboration:** Active partnership between influencer and company

**Campaign:** Company-created opportunity open for applications

**Connection:** Accepted collaboration request

**Deliverable:** Content or service to be provided in collaboration

**Engagement Rate:** Percentage of followers who interact with content

**Match:** AI-recommended potential collaboration partner

**Niche:** Specific content category or industry focus

**Portfolio:** Collection of past work and case studies

**Payout:** Payment transfer to influencer's bank account
