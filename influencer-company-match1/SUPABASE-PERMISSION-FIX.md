# 🔧 SUPABASE PERMISSION FIX

## What You're Seeing

You see this warning:
```
⚠️ You need additional permissions to create a project
Contact your organization owner or administrator for assistance.
```

## Why This Happens

You're trying to create a project in the "Sumano" organization, but you don't have permission to create projects there.

---

## ✅ SOLUTION: Create Your Own Organization

### Step 1: Click the Organization Dropdown
- At the top of the page, you see "Organization" with "Sumano FREE"
- Click on that dropdown (click on "Sumano")

### Step 2: Create New Organization
- You should see options like:
  - "Sumano" (current)
  - "Create a new organization" or "+ New organization"
- Click "Create a new organization"

### Step 3: Name Your Organization
- Enter your name or any name you want (e.g., "MyProjects" or your name)
- Click "Create" or "Continue"

### Step 4: Create Your Project
- Now you'll be back at the "Create a new project" screen
- The organization dropdown should now show YOUR organization
- Fill in:
  - **Project name**: `influencer-match`
  - **Database Password**: Create a strong password (WRITE IT DOWN!)
  - **Region**: Choose closest to you
  - **Pricing plan**: Free
- Click "Create new project"

---

## 🎯 What Happens Next

1. Supabase will create your project (takes 2-3 minutes)
2. You'll see a loading screen
3. Then you'll see your project dashboard
4. Come back here and tell me "Project created!" and we'll continue!

---

## ❓ Can't Find "Create Organization"?

If you don't see an option to create a new organization:

1. Click your profile picture/avatar in the top right corner
2. Look for "Settings" or "Account Settings"
3. Look for "Organizations" section
4. Click "New Organization" or "Create Organization"

---

## 📝 IMPORTANT: Save Your Password!

When you create the database password, write it down somewhere safe! You'll need it to connect your app to the database.

Example password format: `MyApp2024!Secure#Pass`
